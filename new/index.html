About

Madras Eye Films, a media services company based out of Chennai. I have written and directed ads, corporate films, viral videos, promos and behind-the-scenes videos for quite a few clients. I am also a feature film director who has directed one of the five segments of Aviyal, an anthology feature film in Tamil which released in March 2016.
